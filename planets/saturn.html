About Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun, and also the second largest in the Solar System, after Jupiter. And, much like Jupiter, it is also a gas giant, with an iconic "ring" feature surrounding it, composed of billions of pieces of ice, dust and rocks.
