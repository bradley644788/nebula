About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. It is one of the four terrestrial planets within our Solar System, meaning it is characterised by a solid surface and composed of silicate rocks and metals. The surface of Mars is coloured a fusion of orange and red; this is because it is covered in iron oxide dust. It is the second smallest planet in our Solar System after Mercury, with a diameter of 6,779 km (or 4,212 miles) compared to 4,879.4 km (~3,032 miles).
